SLDS Badge Variants Examples


In flow builder, and many others, we are using badges on lines to label connectors in flow products. However there is a need for additional visual styles for errors, connector hover states, and "focus."


In 220 we should have these variants in SLDS, until then we need to roll our own, here are some examples
